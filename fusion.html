About


Fusion is a third-person shooter game with elements of Survival Horror developing by Red Mountain Games studio. Game currently is under active development and suppose to release under PC platform. All in-game footage is a work in progress made with Unreal Engine 4 and does not represent the final look of the game.

World

Your journey begins from the station Black Mountain. Station Black Mountain is a modern military research center created by one of the most powerful corporation called Protonesis. After 2064 there was founded the cave with strange electromagnetic properties. The properties was exactly useful for a station needs. Researches in electromagnetic and biology never be so close to produce next-gen virus based on sound waves propagation and human brain activity.

Gameplay

Fusion designed as classic third-person shooter game with elements of survival. Survival mechanics is created by slow down character movement and resource limiting. Usage of items and environment placed on the station would provide to the players more opportunity to destroy and/or entice enemies. Currently game and mechanics is under active development, stay tuned for our updates.
