ABOUT

Fusion is a sci-fi third-person shooter with the survival horror elements. Fight to survive in the underground science station. Improvise, adapt, overcome. Survival mechanics are based on managing limited resources. Usage of items and environment would provide players with the range of mechanics to destroy and/or lure enemies.

WORLD

Your journey begins from the Black Mountain station. This station is a modern military research center created by one of the most powerful corporations called Protonesis. The cave with strange electromagnetic properties was found there at the beginning of 2064. Its properties came in handy for the station. Researchers in electromagnetism and biology were never so close to create a next-gen virus based on sound waves propagation and human brain activity.

Nevertheless, it all changed in one night. At the beginning you meet Natasha Melnyk — a director of engineering; you both are going to try to figure out what happened and get out of the station.

FEATURES

We are planning implement the range of mechanics such as: the ability to change the gamestyle using surroundings, an electric gun which will be mainly used to solve puzzles, an alpha antagonist and electromagnetic anomalies.

We are planning to let people play for three different characters; each of them has unique mechanics which makes the gameplay more diverting.

GAMEPLAY

Fusion designed as classic third-person shooter game with elements of survival.

Survival mechanics are based on managing limited resources. Usage of items and environment placed on the station would provide to the players more opportunity to destroy and/or entice enemies.

Currently, game and mechanics is under active development, stay tuned for our updates.
