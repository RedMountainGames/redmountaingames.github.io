Who We Are

Red Mountain Games is a game development studio located at Uzhhorod, Ukraine. Our studio is composed of a group of ambitious and creative enthusiasts who want to deliver high-quality narrative and gameplay experience to the players around the world.

Fusion

Fusion is a third-person shooter with elements of Survival Horror. Events takes place at the station Black Mountain - the heart of a modern military research centre directed by scientist Natasha Melnyk. After accident the acient forces has been unleashed. Now Natasha's life won't be the same.

Dead by Design

Dead by Design is a first-person action-adventure game. After a long term of punishment, the Divinus and his partner move to Island to finish their last case. But how exactly the case will end depends on your skills, ability management or stealth. Solve the case and bring the peace to magestic open-world filled of nature.
